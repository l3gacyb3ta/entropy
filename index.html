What could I make?

Try a:

  • Custom Psuedo-random number generator
  • Cellular automoton with random probabilities
  • Shrodinger's cat simulator

Can I join?

Anyone 18 or younger can join! You just have to build something, and track your time.

What counts as a project?

A qualifying project can be anything you want, but it must meet the following conditions:

  • Open Source Forever
  • Included ReadMe.md
  • Fundamentally based in randomness
  • Time spent recorded faithfully through Hackatime
  • Accessible to any user without need of prior experience or setup
How do I track time?

Hack Club uses an in-house time tracking tool to measure and validate time spent on projects. Hackatime supports all major IDEs and text editors, but we also have Lapse for recording timelapses of hardware projects.
