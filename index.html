Get your dice set!!

  • a cellular automoton with random probabilities
  • a Shrodinger's cat simulator

You can make any technical project you want- both software and hardware are accepted!

Can I participate?

Anyone 18 or younger can participate!

What are the project requirements?

Your project should:

  • be open source
  • have a README.md
  • utilize randomization
  • work out of the box (no compiling locally!)
How do I track time?

For software projects, use Hackatime with any text editor of your choice. For hardware projects, use Lapse to screen-record your progress.

Can I submit my project to another Hack Club program?

Double-dipping isn't allowed, sorry!

When does Entropy end?

Entropy runs from June 24, 2026, to July 8, 2026.
